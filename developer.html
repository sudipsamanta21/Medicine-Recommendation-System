Meet the Developer

Developer: SUDIP

I’m a full-stack web developer passionate about building intelligent, user-friendly applications. Health Sphere is a personal project that combines my interests in AI, healthcare, and responsive design.

This app uses Flask for the backend, Bootstrap for the frontend, and a trained SVC model for disease prediction. I’ve integrated multiple datasets to provide users with not just predictions, but also helpful information like precautions, medications, diets, and workouts.

I believe in learning through building. Every bug fixed and every feature added has helped me grow as a developer. If you’d like to collaborate, give feedback, or just say hi—feel free to reach out!

Skills: Flask, Python, Bootstrap, Jinja, Pandas, Machine Learning, Debugging, UI/UX
