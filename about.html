About Health Sphere

Health Sphere is an AI-powered health assistant designed to help users identify potential illnesses based on symptoms they enter. Our system uses machine learning models trained on medical datasets to provide accurate predictions, along with helpful information such as precautions, medications, diet suggestions, and workout routines.

Whether you're feeling unwell or just curious about your symptoms, Health Sphere offers a fast, user-friendly way to get insights and take proactive steps toward better health.

This project is built using Flask, Bootstrap, and a custom-trained SVC model. It reflects our commitment to combining technology and healthcare for a smarter future.

We believe in empowering individuals with knowledge and tools to make informed decisions about their well-being.
